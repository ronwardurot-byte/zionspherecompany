About Us

Zion Sphere Company is a data intelligence company specializing in analytics, pricing insights, and technology solutions for the US live events market.

We empower companies with real-time data, smart tools, and market-driven insights that support better decisions and stronger revenue outcomes.

Operating through two legally registered entities under one and the same ownership:

  • Zion Sphere LLC (United States) – US Transacting company for strategy, marketplace relations and technology development
  • Zion Sphere Analytics Corp. (Philippines) – PH Operating company for analytics, research, offshore operations, and market intelligence

Together, we combine global talent, advanced data engineering, and industry expertise to deliver reliable intelligence across the live events ecosystem.
